Artificial Intelligence

Explore intelligent agents, search algorithms, knowledge representation, machine learning fundamentals, and natural language processing.

16 Weeks 4 Credits Dr. Rajesh Kumar

Syllabus

  1. Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
  2. Intelligent Agents
  3. Problem Solving & Search Techniques
  4. Knowledge Representation
  5. Reasoning & Inference
  6. Machine Learning Basics
  7. Natural Language Processing
  8. Ethics & Applications of AI

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand core concepts and applications of AI
  • Design and analyze intelligent agents
  • Apply search and reasoning techniques to real-world problems
  • Gain foundational knowledge of machine learning and NLP
